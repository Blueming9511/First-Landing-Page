MY FAVORITE ALBUMS OF ALL TIME

Reputation

Taylor Swift's sixth studio album, "reputation", was released on November 10, 2017. It marked a significant shift in her musical style and public persona. The album's themes revolve around Swift's experiences with public scrutiny, betrayal, and the media, reflecting her personal growth and newfound confidence in the aftermath of several controversies.

EYES WIDE OPEN

Eyes Wide Open was a critical and commercial success, debuting at number one on the Gaon Album Chart and the Billboard World Albums Chart. The album also received positive reviews from critics, who praised its diverse sound and Twice's vocal performances.
